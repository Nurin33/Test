WHAT IS PHP

PHP is a programming languange that can do all sorts of things: evaluate from data sent from a browser, build custom web content to serve the browser, talk to a database, and even send and receive cookies (little packets of data that your browser uses to remember things, like if you're logged in to Codecademy).
